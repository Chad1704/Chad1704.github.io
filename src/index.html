About Me

As someone born into the era when personal computers became household staples, I've always been drawn to them. My fascination began with experimenting in creating maps for games like Counter-Strike, which eventually led me to teaching myself 3D modeling and programming. This journey has brought me to Humber College, where I'm eager to further expand my knowledge.


Graduating from school with a High Skills Major specialization in engineering, I realized that while I appreciated the technical aspects, my true passion lay in programming. After high school, I decided to explore cybersecurity at Fanshawe, but found it wasn't the right fit for me. I returned to Toronto to delve deeper into my passion for computer programming.


I'm still in the process of discovering which area of development I want to specialize in, and currently, I'm exploring a variety of fields. In my free time, you'll find me either immersed in video games, spending time with friends, or enjoying nature walks.
